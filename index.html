About Me

After developing his craft as a lead guitarist and vocalist in Toronto’s pop-punk scene, Daniel Meschino has evolved into a singer-songwriter in his own right. Influenced by artists such as John Mayer, Justin Bieber, and Shawn Mendes, the Vaughan, Ontario native is focused on creating pop music that is both inspiring and relatable, while remaining authentic.

His journey took a pivotal turn after a chance meeting with Steve Molella—producer, songwriter, and drummer for Canadian rock band Finger Eleven. Together, they spent the latter half of 2020 working on Daniel’s long-awaited debut EP, blending introspective lyrics and emotional vocals with bright, upbeat production.

Daniel Meschino’s debut single, “ALONE,” highlights his smooth falsetto, emotional delivery, and growing versatility as an artist.
