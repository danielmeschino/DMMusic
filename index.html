About Me

After cutting his teeth as a lead guitarist and vocalist in Toronto, Canada’s pop-punk scene, Daniel Meschino has evolved into a singer-songwriter in his own right.

Influenced by John Mayer, Justin Bieber, and Shawn Mendes, he creates pop music that is authentic, relatable, and emotionally driven.

His debut single “ALONE” showcases his smooth falsetto and growing artistry.
