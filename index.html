About

After developing his craft as a guitarist and vocalist in Toronto’s pop-punk scene, Daniel Meschino has evolved into a singer-songwriter creating emotional and relatable pop music. Influenced by John Mayer, Justin Bieber, and Shawn Mendes, his sound blends storytelling with modern production.
